CSR - Open Opportunities for Youth of Differences (YoD) organised by Rotary District 3450.

AsiaPay has participated in a meaningful CSR project "Open Opportunities for Youth of Differences (YoD)" organised by Rotary District 3450, offered a workplace experience with not less than 14 hours on 13 and 15 April 2021 to those with special needs which supports disabilities inclusion. Through this partnership, AsiaPay hopes to strengthen the community by helping to welcome people with developmental disabilities into the fullness of community life.
