JOB DESCRIPTION

Responsibilities:

• Manage relationship with key merchants, banks and partners on merchant operation and new product partnerships;
• Supervise merchant onboarding, KYC process and merchant sales and activities monitoring;
• Develop and manage Service Management programs;

• Possess strong interpersonal skills and able to work well with all levels;
• Details-oriented, good interpersonal skills and able to work independently;
• Excellent problem-solving skills;
• Able to work under pressure and tight deadline;
• Proficient in both spoken and written English.
