Innovation in Retail Payment Service

To embrace the new market trend, we offer you the latest and most advanced POS solution to meet your retail businesses of any sizes.

PayPOS by PayDollar

PayPOS by PayDollar offers complete retail payment solutions one-stop. It provides packaged multiple payment methods that offers total payment flexibility and enables merchants to accept payments on-the-go subject to various devices. Support a wide range of advanced payment options, as well as inbuilt NFC contactless, electronic signature capture.

The device comes with the mPOS Plus mobile application that allows merchants to process payments in real-time, view transaction history and sales reports.
